SPAM MESSAGE

Spam messages are unsolicited messages sent in bulk, typically for commercial purposes, and can include emails, text messages, and social media messages. They are often sent to a large number of people without their permission, and can be annoying, intrusive, and potentially harmful.

Spam messages are usually generated automatically by spamming software, and they can be difficult to filter or block. However, many email services and messaging platforms use filtering algorithms that can help reduce the amount of spam messages that users receive.

SPAM MESSAGE TIPS

  • Be cautious about sharing your personal information online, especially on websites or platforms that you are not familiar with.
  • Use spam filters and antivirus software on your computer and mobile devices.
  • Don't respond to or click on any links in spam messages, as they could be malicious or lead to phishing scams.
  • Block or report any suspicious messages or accounts that send you spam.
  • Avoid opening attachments or downloading files from unknown sources.
